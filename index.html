A Word From Leadership

Message from the Director

Director
“

Every student who walks through our doors is walking away from a certificate mentality and into an enterprise mindset.

When we started Annita Technical Training Institute, the goal was never just to teach a skill and say goodbye. We wanted to build something one of a kind in Africa — a place where a young person from Kenol, or anywhere else, could learn a trade, open a bank account, receive start-up capital, and walk out with an actual business plan in hand. That is the promise we make to every learner who enrolls with us.

I meet our students at their level — whatever their background, whatever their pace — and I make sure our coaches and instructors do the same. Skills open doors, but it is discipline, saving culture, and real business support that keep those doors open long after graduation. That is what we are building here, one learner at a time.

The Director Annita Technical Training Institute
